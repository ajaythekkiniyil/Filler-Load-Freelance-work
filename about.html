Welcome to FillerLoad

Filler load platform will help you to find extra load used to utilize the vacant/ unutilized space in a vehicle carrying consignments. The platform also enables you to share any additional load to more than 1000 vendors in your region.

What drives us?

The growth of tech in the past decade has enabled us to think big and explore new possibilities. Filler Load aims to take advantage of the cutting-edge technology in the area of programming machine learning and artificial intelligence.

 Supporting Small Businesses

Due to increase profit margins, vendors will be able to provide best in industry rate to the small business who wants to move their material. This will be a big support to entrepreneurs and budding businesses.

Committed for a better future

Burning of fossil fuel is one of the key reason for global warming. The rate of fuel consumption is increasing year on year. Complete utilization of space in a vehicle will help in reducing the carbon footprint of the logistics and transportation industry.

Load Carrier

Load Carrier module can be used to declare vacant space and find suitable providers. Transport service providers including fleet owners, brokers, vendors, 3PL companies or any other who transports shipments for their clients can use this module to find individuals or companies who can provide load. This load from the market will help you to fill your vacant space. You can provide your best rate and close the deal faster. Filler load mobile platform includes more than thousands of such parties who can provide you load.

Load Provider

Any Individual/ small business owner or companies who have up to 5000 kg of load to transport can use Load Provider Module to share available load and find carriers. The easy navigation through the dashboard and other functionalities makes sharing load much easier and faster. Our national level support team is available for you around the clock to answer your queries. You can find choose the best price from the market and the carrier of your choice. From now on you have the full control on whom you are providing your load to.

Cost Bench Marking & Star rating

You can write genuine rating based on the experience you had with your shipment partner. As a customer centric platform, your reviews will be analysed and reviewed by our rating management team. You can find best reviewed transporters from the market. FillerLoad provides with the best price from the market. You can get in touch with the vendor or load provider and agree upon the suitable price for you.
